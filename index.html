Tawheed: The Oneness of Allah

Tawheed is the most fundamental concept in Islam. It means believing that Allah is One, with no partners, and He alone deserves worship.

The Five Pillars of Islam

Shahada, Salah, Zakat, Sawm, and Hajj form the foundation of a Muslim's life, guiding their spiritual and social responsibilities.

Following the Sunnah

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) is the best role model. Following his Sunnah helps us live a life that is pleasing to Allah.
